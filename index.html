Is the internet dead?

September 27, 2018


With the new laws set in place by the EU that restricts copyright content on website it
brings up the question, is the internet dead? Ever since net neutrality was killed in late 2017
the future of the internet was unknown but with the EU's new law that restricts copyright content on websites like giphy, instagram, facebook and more it really makes you wonder, whats going to happen to the internet. Heres what im guessing what will happen to the internet:
-major sites will shut down due to lack of advertising -less jobs avaliable -lower stock prices -companys will shut down -less news outlets...
I honestly think the EU should reconsider theyre choice because this law could also effect things outside of the internet.
